Podcast Episodes I Recommend

  • 10x Your Bitcoin Security with Multisig

    Stephan Livera sits down with Michael Flaxman to talk about the power of multisig for securing your Bitcoin private keys. I re-listen to this episode often.

  • Nic Carter: Bitcoin Core Values, Layered Scaling, and Blocksize Debates | Lex Fridman Podcast

    All bitcoin everything

  • The science of weight loss with Kevin Hall, PhD

    What conclusions we can draw from the research, what future research we still need, and what anyone can do, now, to become healthier and lighter.

  • Building muscle, losing fat, and the importance of resistance training

    Peter Attia and Layne Norton talk about strength training, navigating injuries, and nutrition science. These are two people obsessed with getting the context right - something I think is lost in much of our discussions around health and evidence in nutition studies.

  • Tim Ferris, Nick Szabo, and Naval Ravikant

    BTC and other endlessly interesting ideas. A monster of an episode.

  • Biology & Culture: A coversation with Bret Weinstein

    Bret Weinstein and Sam Harris cover the Evergreen fiasco, population and sex differences, as well as the utility of metaphorical truth.

  • Mitochondria control cancer and immunity

    Not a podcast, but a great presentation by Navdeep Chandel about the link between metabolism and immunology.
